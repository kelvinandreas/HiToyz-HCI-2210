OUR BACKGROUND HISTORY

HI-Toyz is a toy store that has been in operation since 2019, specializing in the sale of plastic model kits and figure statues. Over the years, HI-Toyz has become an important part of the local collector toy community, providing a wide range of toys from various brands and serving as a center for toy collector activities.

Since its inception, HI-Toyz has become a favorite destination for collectors of all ages who are passionate about their hobby. The store has built a strong reputation for carrying a diverse selection of toys from leading brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel Toys, Mini-GT, and more. HI-Toyz has also established itself as an official reseller for these top brands, offering collectors the latest and most sought-after toys.

HI-Toyz has come a long way since its early days. Starting out as a small business, the store has grown in popularity and become one of the most prominent toy stores in the local area. Its reputation as a one-stop-shop for all things related to model kits and figures has earned it a special place in the hearts of local collectors.

As part of the local toy collector community, HI-Toyz has become a hub for collector activities, hosting events and gatherings that bring together enthusiasts from all over the region. The store has become a focal point for collectors looking to meet others who share their passion, exchange ideas, and learn about the latest trends and products in the toy industry.

In summary, HI-Toyz is more than just a toy store. It is a community hub that has played an important role in the growth and development of the local collector toy community. With its extensive selection of products and commitment to serving collectors of all ages, HI-Toyz has become a beloved institution among toy collectors in the region.
